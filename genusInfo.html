Canis Species Information
Dog
Dog Traits
  • Loyal to their owner.
  • Loving to the one who feeds them.
  • Playful, especially when around other dogs.
Conservation status: of least concern.
Wolf
Wolf Traits
  • Extremely intelligent.
  • Non-agressive (contrary to popular belief).
  • Able to make strong emotional connections with others.
Conservation status: of least concern.
Coyote
Coyote Traits
  • Very sneaky and clever.
  • Extremely shy.
  • Very loyal to their pack or family.
Conservation status: of least concern.
Golden Jackal
Golden Jackal Traits
  • Very loyal to their mate.
  • They travel with their family unit only, not in large packs.
  • Very adaptable to whatever environment they are in.
Conservation status: of least concern.
Dire Wolf
Dire Wolf Traits
  • Hypercarnivorous.
  • Hunted in packs.
  • However hypercarnivorous, they mostly consumed herbivores.
Conservation status: extinct
Red Wolf
Red Wolf Traits
  • Many of the young wolves spend time alone or in small non-breeding packs composed of unrelated individuals.
  • Denning sites include hollow tree trunks, along stream banks and the abandoned earths of other animals.
  • Semi-sociable in context to other breeds of wolves
Conservation status: of least concern.
